Our Three Pillars

Cultural Preservation

We archive stories, document community history, and create permanent records of cultural significance. Our master-apprentice programs ensure knowledge from Fantasy Records era masters is not lost.

Youth Development

Through AAFC (Artists and Athletes for Change), we house youth leadership programs that train young people in communication, digital skills, and civic engagement‚Äîcreating alternative pathways beyond traditional college routes.

Artist Support

Our AAIR (Artist Architect in Residence) program provides structure and mentorship for artists working in heritage and community, centered at Historic Jones Memorial United Methodist Church in San Francisco.
